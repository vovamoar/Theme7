FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is your honey harvested?

Our honey is harvested using traditional methods that ensure the highest quality. We carefully extract honey from the combs without heating it, preserving all natural enzymes and beneficial properties.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship our honey products worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. All international orders are carefully packed to ensure safe delivery.

How long does honey last?

Pure honey never spoils when properly stored! Keep it in a sealed container at room temperature. It may crystallize over time, which is natural and can be reversed by gentle warming.

Are your products organic?

Yes, all our honey products are certified organic. Our bees forage in pristine areas free from pesticides and artificial chemicals, ensuring the purest honey possible.
