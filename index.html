FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is your food harvested?

Our food is harvested using traditional methods that ensure the highest quality. We carefully extract food from the farms without heating it, preserving all natural enzymes and beneficial properties.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship our food products worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. All international orders are carefully packed to ensure safe delivery.

How long does food last?

Pure food never spoils when properly stored! Keep it in a sealed info at room temperature. It may crystallize over time, which is natural and can be reversed by gentle warming.

Are your products organic?

Yes, all our food products are certified organic. Our farmers forage in pristine areas free from pesticides and artificial chemicals, ensuring the purest food possible.

What makes your food different from store-bought food?

Unlike mass-produced food, our products are raw and sourced from local farmers. We never heat-treat our food, which preserves all the natural enzymes, pollen, and beneficial compounds.
